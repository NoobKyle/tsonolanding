Our Story

Tsono was born from a simple frustration: group rides shouldn't be stressful. We've all been there—separated at a red light, unsure if everyone made the turn, or unable to warn the group about a hazard ahead.

Existing apps weren't built for riders. They were made for families tracking kids or delivery drivers. We needed something designed for the road, for gloves, for the split-second decisions we make at 60mph.

So we built Tsono—a real-time companion that keeps your crew connected without the friction. No app stores, no complicated setup. Just open your browser and ride.

"The road is better together"

— Tsono Team

What We Believe

Rider First

Every feature is designed with gloves on and helmets buckled. If it doesn't work at 60mph, it doesn't ship.

Instant, Not Later

Real-time means real-time. 3-second updates, instant signals, zero lag. When safety matters, speed matters.

Privacy First

Your location is yours. Share only when you choose, leave anytime. No persistent tracking when you're not riding.

Community Driven

Built with rider feedback, shaped by the community. We ride too, and we listen.

Zero Friction

No app store, no accounts for guests, no download. Share a link and they're in. That's how it should be.

Safety Obsessed

Hazard alerts, emergency signals, screen wake lock. We obsess over the features that keep you safe.
