Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tsono really free?

Yes! The free tier is free forever with no catches. Join unlimited rides, use all signals, and track your crew in real-time. We only charge for power features like larger groups and ride history.

Can I try paid plans before committing?

Absolutely. All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What happens if I downgrade?

You keep access to your ride history, but creating new rides will be limited to your new plan's capacity. Your data is never deleted.

Do I need to download an app?

No! Tsono is a Progressive Web App. Open it in your browser, add to home screen, and you're ready. No app store needed.

Can our whole club share one Crew account?

The Crew plan is for one account holder who can create unlimited rides. Each rider still uses their own free or paid account to join rides.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay through Stripe's secure payment system.
